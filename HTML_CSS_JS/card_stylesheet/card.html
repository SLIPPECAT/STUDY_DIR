Seoul of Korea

Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a city that seamlessly blends ancient traditions with modern innovation. Known for its dynamic cityscape, iconic landmarks like Dongdaemun Design Plaza, and rich cultural heritage, Seoul is a vibrant hub of creativity and history.

Tokyo of Japan

Tokyo, Japan's capital, represents a harmonious fusion of tradition and futuristic advancements. The city features a juxtaposition of ancient temples like Senso-ji with neon-lit skyscrapers in districts like Shibuya and Shinjuku. Tokyo serves as a global technological and cultural hub, with a strong emphasis on precision and craftsmanship.

New York of USA

New York City, a global financial powerhouse, is an iconic metropolis that embodies the American dream. The city's skyline, dominated by landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, reflects its global influence. NYC is renowned for its cultural institutions like Broadway and the Metropolitan Museum of Art, as well as its diverse neighborhoods that showcase a rich tapestry of cultures.
